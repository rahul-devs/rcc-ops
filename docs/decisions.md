# Architecture Decision Records

## Purpose

This document indexes Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) for RCC-OPS. ADRs capture significant technical decisions, their context, and consequences.

## Conventions

- ADRs are numbered sequentially (ADR-001, ADR-002, …)
- Status: Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded
- Each ADR is self-contained with context, decision, and consequences
