# Workflows

## Purpose

This document describes the operational workflows supported by RCC-OPS. Each workflow defines the sequence of steps, actors, and system interactions involved in a business process.

## Conventions

- Workflows are identified by a stable ID (e.g. `WF-001`)
- Each workflow includes: trigger, actors, steps, outcomes, and related events
- Do not document workflows here until they are formally defined
